How to use this

Edit a chat or call — Tap any row to change its name, message, time, ticks, unread badge or photo, or to delete it.

Add rows — Tap the green + on Chats or Calls (or the ••• menu) to add a new chat or call.

Reorder — Press and hold a chat or call for a moment, then drag it to a new spot.

Photos — When choosing a photo you can drag the circle to frame the face, like a real profile picture.

You tab — Tap your name or picture to set your own name, photo, status bubble, the Chats tab badge and the Archived count.

Presets — In the ••• menu, save the whole setup under a name and switch between saved setups instantly.

Saving — Everything saves automatically on this device.

Realism tip — Add this page to your Home Screen (Share → Add to Home Screen) so it opens full-screen with no browser bar.
