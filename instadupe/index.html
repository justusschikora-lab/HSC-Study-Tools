How to use this

Edit profile — Tap Edit profile to set your name, username, bio, the note bubble above your photo, follower & following counts, profile picture and the DM badge number.

Add content — Tap the + at the top-left to add a Post, Reel, Highlight, Mention or Repost. You can pick several photos at once and frame each one. Reposts get their own tab once you add one.

Add highlights — Tap the New circle to choose a cover photo, crop it, and give it an emoji or title.

Remove things — Tap a post to open it and hit Remove; tap a highlight to edit or remove it.

Reorder — Press and hold a post or highlight for a moment, then drag it to a new spot.

Tabs — Switch between Posts, Videos and Mentions. Add a repost from the + menu and the Reposts tab appears.

Saving — Everything saves automatically on this device.

Presets — Tap the lock icon by your name to save the whole profile (details, posts and highlights) as a named preset, and switch between saved profiles instantly.

Start over — In Edit profile, use Reset profile or Start from scratch.
